A Message from our President

Hello, and thank you for your interest in the Epsilon Sigma Chapter of Alpha Kappa Psi at the Georgia Institute of Technology. For the last 53 years, our Chapter has worked to build and strengthen Georgia Tech's business community through numerous events, workshops, and conferences. Today we continue that mission.

We are the business powerhouse of Georgia Tech. We work to bring together Tech's highest achieving, business-minded students to foster a community of growth and success. With over 60 members spanning all majors, all disciplines, and all cultures at Tech, Alpha Kappa Psi consistently ranks among the highest achieving organizations, in academics, job opportunities, and campus involvement. Boasting an average GPA 0.3 points higher than Georgia Tech's average and alumni working America's top firms such as Google, Amazon, Microsoft, Goldman Sachs, Deloitte, and Accenture, Alpha Kappa Psi serves as Atlanta's hearth of student success and professional leadership.

If this is your first time visiting, I encourage you to explore the information available here. Whether you're a company looking to partner with us or a student interested in joining, please feel free to email me at president@gtakpsi.com. I can honestly say that becoming a Brother of Alpha Kappa Psi has been one of the defining moments of my life allowing me to truly see my own potential. Founded upon a community that constantly strives for personal and professional improvement, Alpha Kappa Psi possesses a magic like no other.

- Murtaza Bambot, President - Epsilon Sigma Chapter

About AKΨ

In 1904, Alpha Kappa Psi was founded on the principles of educating its members and the public to appreciate and demand higher ideals in business and to further the individual welfare of members during college and beyond.

Young professionals everywhere are discovering that Alpha Kappa Psi is much more than just another organization or club – it is a unique, prestigious association of students, professors, graduates and professionals with common interests and goals. They join Alpha Kappa Psi to take advantage of valuable educational, friendship and networking opportunities.

Professionalism

Alpha Kappa Psi is recognized as the premier developer of principled business leaders.

Our chapter continuously strives to develop our members and the Georgia Tech community to appreciate and demand higher ideals in business and professionalism. We supplement our members' college education with high-quality professional development and events.

Our members receive resume critiquing services, interview skills development, public speaking opportunities, time management and communication skills that prepare them incredibly well to tackle the challenges of entering into, and prospering in, the business community.

Philanthropy

Alpha Kappa Psi realizes how important it is to give back to the community. Every semester, brothers collectively donate hundreds of hours to various causes around the Georgia Tech community and broader. Our brotherhood participates in Team Buzz, Tech Beautification Day, Relay for Life, MedShare, Sheltering Arms, and local park clean-up's, to name a few.

Leadership

Membership in Alpha Kappa Psi provides countless leadership development opportunities. Every member is encouraged to take on leadership roles within the chapter, the Georgia Tech community, and the greater global society. The guidance, support, and opportunities available to our members ensure that they are well equipped with the skills needed to effectively lead with enthusiasm and integrity.

Social Events

Although AKΨ is a professional fraternity, we believe a balance of business and social life is important. Throughout the semester, we host several social events for Brothers:

Blue Sapphire Formal

In the Fall, our main event is Blue Sapphire, which is a formal held in the Atlanta area. For the dinner and dance, Brothers get dressed up in formal wear and dance the night away. This night is special in several ways, including Brother, Pledge, and Alumni bonding, and superlative events. Through superlatives, we recognize brothers who go above and beyond the call of duty for the Fraternity, as well as fun recognitions such as best dressed Brother and most charming Brother.

Yellow Rose Semi-Formal

During the Spring we hold a Yellow Rose Semi-Formal outside of Atlanta. For instance, last year our semi-formal was held in Destin, Florida. Brothers and Pledges spent their weekend relaxing on the beach followed up by a Hawaiian themed semi formal at night. Yellow Rose Semi-Formal is definitely a memorable trip that tends to be everyone's favorite every year. The best stories come out of Yellow Rose Semi-Formal and it's one event that you can't possibly miss!

Other Events

Other events we hold include after chapter dinner, pre-meeting socials for Brother and Pledge bonding, and other events such as paintball and going to Georgia Tech football/basketball games (Go Tech!). So, although our main focus is not to be a social Fraternity, our bonds grow deeper than business through events such as these where we are able to just hang out and have fun with each other.
